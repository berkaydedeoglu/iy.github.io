    Get Ready for the Future with Digital Transformation!

    ST INDUSTRY 4.0 JOURNAL INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

    03 October 2018

     

    THE JOURNAL OF ST INDUSTRY 4.0

    INTERVIEW QUESTIONS

     

    ST: How do your products serve the digital transformation of the industry? Can we learn your company vision in this sense?

     

    We as İletişim Yazılım have been producing Smart Business Products for 25 years, that will improve digital maturity levels and increase their productivity in their transition journeys to Industry 4.0 where they set out to increase their competitiveness. We develop Smart Business Products to enhance digital maturity levels and increase productivity.

     

    We produce Manufacturing Management Software known as MES (Manufacturing Execution System) in the world. With the collection of instant data from the production area, we aim to manage the actual costs of the enterprises. We help enterprises to minimize their costs by increasing productivity. One of our most important goals is to provide enterprises with instant data and reduce their costs with CoralReef, our MES product, which is also included in CGI's MES Product Survey.

     

    With our ManageMind software, our BPM (Business Process Management) solution, we enable businesses to create organizational culture and digitize in a computer environment. With this software, paper usage is minimized and enterprises can monitor all process stages and KPI (Key Performance Indicators). Thus, bottleneck points can be monitored by monitoring process performance. In addition, all documented works required by ISO standards can be performed quickly and safely with our software.

    Our vision is to become the leader in MES and BPM in the national market. Our company, which has been working in this direction for 25 years, is increasing its power with new references day by day.

     

    ST: What topics will you focus on at the Industry 4.0 Applications Summit and Exhibition?

     

    Smart Business Solutions is our main strategy and we aim to inform the participants with our sample projects with our software developed with current technologies.

    With our CoralReef MES product, we aim to share our solutions for collecting and managing digital data mainly on Machine Scheduling, Quality Control, Shop Floor Control, Maintenance Management, Mold / Tool Management, Warehouse and Shipment Management. With CoralReef, we aim to provide information on how to ensure efficiency and traceability, what kind of analysis can be performed, and how to monitor essential Performance Indicators for businesses such as OEE, PPM.

     

    With our ManageMind product, we will share in the process of digitizing processes such as Process Management, Document Management, Asset Management, Project Management, Human Resources Management, Performance Management and Survey Management.

     

    ST:      Which sectors are your goals?

    With our ManageMind product, we will share in the process of digitizing processes such as Process Management, Document Management, Asset Management, Project Management, Human Resources Management, Performance Management and Survey Management.

     

    ST:      Which products / solutions will you present at the Summit regarding these sectors? Can you tell us about the successful Industry 4.0 applications developed by your brand? 

    We will introduce our Smart Business Solutions at the Summit. We will introduce CoralReef, the MES solution, ManageMind, the BPM solution, and IMT, our Integration tool. We also aim to transfer our specific solutions that we have developed in areas such as demand forecasting, order transfer, contract management, configuration management.

     

    We aim to transfer our successful projects with our CoralReef product, which enables factories to manage their processes digitally and instantly, from planning to shipment, integration with different ERPs, and communicating with machines. Technologies supported by CoralReef like barcode, data matrix, RFID and so on get faster, instant, error-free data entry is possible. With PLC integration; Besides the basic production information such as number of production, stop information, cycle time of different types and models of machines, process parameters such as temperature, pressure, speed of rotation, welding temperature and regeneration value are presented to the users through the reporting and analysis module of our software.

    With traceability, it is possible for enterprises to perform backwards analysis and identify the source of the problem.

    With OEE analysis, total equipment efficiency can be reported by the system and detailed analysis of losses can be performed over the system.

     

    ST:      : According to you, what will be the Industry 4.0 Application Summit and Exhibition in Turkey contributed to the industries? What are your expectations?

     

    In today's world where competition is becoming more and more challenging, the transition to Industry 4.0 has become inevitable for businesses. In order to compete with the companies in the global market, it is among the priorities of enterprises to realize customer-specific production, to provide production with accurate and minimum cost by digitizing the processes.

    In this context, it will be critical and important that solutions and businesses come together and become partners.

    Especially in this period where the importance of localization is understood more, introducing national solutions to enterprises is of great importance.

    In this event, our biggest expectation is to bring our solutions together with businesses and exchange information.

     

    ST:      What are your suggestions for expanding the market?

     

    It is important for companies to deliver the solutions they produce in Industry 4.0 with achievement stories to the enterprises.

    In particular, what the enterprises will gain through the analysis of productivity and losses, and what gains they can achieve by managing their actual costs are of great importance for the national economy. In today's world, where international competition is becoming increasingly difficult, businesses have to focus on productivity.

    Efficiency studies are a fact that requires continuity for every enterprise.

    In order to prioritize, plan and evaluate the results of the studies to be carried out in this context, productivity must be continuously monitored with real data. Therefore, it is very important for enterprises to establish MES systems.

    In addition, it is of great importance for the country and the market that the importance of digitalization and the fact that it becomes a necessity is understood more by the industry. Such summits are therefore critical to the sharing and reproduction of information.
