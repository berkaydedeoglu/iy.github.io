    This Year Promotion, Next Year Export!

    İletişim Yazılım, which works to increase the productivity of companies, plans to open the door of export with its projects in the field of R & D. Tuncer Hatunoğlu, Chairman of the Board of İletişim Yazılım, explaining that they are expecting the products to mature until today; we see 2019 as a preparatory year especially in terms of publicity. Our goal is to export as of 2020, "he said.

    24 August 2019

     

    İletişim Yazılım was established in Bursa 25 years ago and is operating in the field of information systems that increase productivity. Identifying the problems experienced by the manufacturing companies, the company puts forth studies to minimize the losses. The company closely follows the developments in the sector and carries out original works in this field. İletişim Yazılım, which previously offered separate projects to each enterprise, faced Manufacturing Execution System (MES) after 2007 and signed different software. CoralReef software which is the first in Turkey and then Bursa offers partnership solutions to companies operating in the production areas in general.

    They founded R & D in 2018

    Tuncer Hatunoğlu, Chairman of İletişim Yazılım Board, explained that they serve in Gazcılar 550 and 50 square meters area in Ulutek and reminded that the R & D center was officially launched in October 2018. Hatunoğlu said that they provide employment for 50 people throughout the company and 35 of them are R & D employees and added that they are composed of electrical, mechatronics, computer and industrial engineers.

    Emphasizing that they attach importance to teamwork, Hatunoğlu said that they get successful results with employees in different disciplines. Stating that they have developed a methodology in the company over the years, Hatunoğlu, R & D is also a discipline job. I'm not talking about working hour discipline. Working discipline and to share information, research, requests on this issue are important, "he said.

    Defining Industry 4.0 as a 'project to increase productivity by using new technologies in industry', Hatunoğlu said that this completely coincided with his works. Hatunoğlu pointed out the importance of being able to measure himself in order to increase the efficiency of a company and he explained his works using CoralReef.

    VERTICAL PRODUCTIVITY

    Hatunoğlu was explaining that they have demonstrated their efficiency by managing the factory site and resources primarily in the system they have developed, reminding that there are situations that are overlooked while doing uniform work, after our determinations, we have projects that we put forward in vertical productivity ace. From an example we have, you have a large warehouse. 10 forklifts come and go, take the materials in place and put them to the destination. The operator works at the head of each forklift. On the other hand, things do not grow, the material is waiting! You say. Here we have developed a vertical solution for forklift management. Which forklift is where? Where do you need it? When did he/she let me know it was empty? Where is the material waiting? By matching them, we're going to go directly to the forklift operator Now go to 'Do this'. This project is being implemented. This operation will be able to do the same job more quickly and with 5 forklifts. This is one of the important projects we can add in the name of efficiency, "he said.

    INDUSTRY 4.0

    Underlining the necessity of analyzing all the productivity in the field before it can be done, Hatunoğlu said that the human data working in the field is entering the system. We put a device here and know the product of the machine according to the work order from the planning during the product 'Is the right product? According to the plan, am I doing this? ' issues. If he does, he's going to approve the operation of the machine. Otherwise the machine will not work. So there is no need to tell the worker extra 'Enter Data'. The material entering here is all tagged electronically. You can see whether the data is loaded into the main system by the machine and whether the production envisaged by the planning side is actually performed in the field. This is one of the things Industry 4.0 wants. We got the patent. Our work in this area continues on one hand, " said Hatunoglu.

    WE ARE AT THE POINT ABLE TO COPMPETE ABROAD

    Hatunoğlu was explaining that they give importance to artificial intelligence, said they are working on decision-making mechanisms. Hatunoğlu reminded that the company is working in the domestic market, said that they expect the products to mature so far. Stating that the increase in foreign exchange in recent years shows that they can be more competitive abroad, Hatunoğlu said: We have continuously measured our own efficiency in terms of service delivery. We have developed our team accordingly. We have prepared our own information systems accordingly. When we take these services abroad, we have seen that we can compete. We see 2019 as a preparatory year, especially in terms of publicity. We are developing our cooperation with the University of Arizona in the USA and Aachen University in Germany. Our goal is to export as of 2020, "he said.

    BOTTLENECK QUALIFIED MANPOWER

    That is very valuable R & D incentives in Turkey what Hatunoğlu stressed, noting the importance of the protection of qualified human resources, measures should be taken for the return of brain drain. There are serious incentives in R & D. Our country has no choice but has to do R & D. There is also a consciousness here, but the bottleneck remained in the qualified manpower, "he said.

    AN INCREASE OF PRODUCTIVITY BY 8 to 10 % IS A SERIOUS INCREASE.

    Hatunoğlu also gives an example from a work they do on looms, indicating that one of the most important problems in drawing and knot operation is the weaving loom, for example, 500-1000 cycles. Warp at the back, attaching here when the material is finished; it takes 1-1.5 hours to make a new one. In an enterprise with close to 100 machines, it is critical that these warps and empty machines do not remain. We developed software for the weaving mills that can follow the warp management.

     

    Hürriyet, 24.08.2019
