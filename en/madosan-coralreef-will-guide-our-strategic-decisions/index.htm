    Madosan: "CoralReef will guide our strategic decisions!"

    CoralReef will guide our strategic decisions!

    03 September 2019

    With Industry 4.0; IoT (Internet of Things), AR (Augmented Reality), VR (Virtual Reality), AI (Artificial Intelligence), drones, autonomous tools, and even Brainternet (the simultaneous connection of the human brain to the Internet) are now the future of the world. It will be much more difficult for institutions that still act with a traditional approach, ignoring the human being know that this is the case. As Madosan Shelf Systems, we continue to take important steps towards technology with the slogan of Becoming a Professional Family. We searched for a platform where we can monitor every stage and every stage of the internet, talk with our investment in paint shop and monitor other stages of production. As a result of our research; we have chosen İletişim Yazılım as our solution provider because of its long years of experience, vision and competent and professional staff.

    • What are our primary expectations from MES software?

    MES (Manufacturing Executive Systems) must differentiate itself from the part software or ERP systems used in different units of production in the enterprise, providing simultaneous and standard traceability. Turkey and the standards of ERP systems in the world stage as it is known, according to the areas which exhibit the necessary flexibility and dynamism of the manufacturing sector needs MES plays a key role. At this point, it is a great advantage that a MES software enables fast, but at the same time, data collection from the field in a user-friendly display design that restricts incorrect data entry. In addition, the flexible reporting capabilities of all authorized personnel to access the same information from the same point makes decision-making processes faster, better quality and personal.

    When we do research about companies that implement MES applications, we participate in various events where we listen to success stories, we have seen through face-to-face interviews with user companies that such systems reduce data entry times and data loss, and ensure that production performance can be realized in an instant to prevent production loss. As such, it has vital benefits for a business. Our expectations are to implement all of these benefits and more.

    • Why did we choose İletişim Yazılım?

    Among the companies that we interviewed, İletişim Yazılım came to the forefront as a software company with a corporate approach with its 25 years of history, recognition in the sector and experiences that can be exemplary in different sectors. İletişim Yazılım has given us the confidence that the project can be managed in a healthy way from the beginning to the end because it is predominantly analytical thinking industrial engineers with different perspectives. In terms of products, we thought that a structure that can show flexibility to different sectors over a single product can also meet our needs.

    The fact that the CoralReef reporting tool currently includes more than 200 reports and the ability to develop our own reports has been a major factor in our preference for İletişim Yazılım.

    In addition, CoralReef offers us detailed modules where ERP programs such as operational quality and mold management, planning and monitoring of maintenance processes have lost their flexibility made us attractive.

    • What do we aim with the CoralReef MES system?

    With CoralReef system, we determined the modules we want to manage in the first stage in the field. We will ensure that modules such as machine planning, input-process-final quality control processes, material flow in production, management of unplanned failures and planned maintenance, time and quantity based tracking of molds, scale automation are integrated with our ERP system.

    In this way, we will transfer machine-labor periods, resource consumption such as materials, which constitute the basic cost elements in the field, to the ERP cost module that we are actively using thanks to CoralReef ERP integration on the basis of work order and cost center. We foresee that the simple and clear flow of information to our cost system provides a reliable basis for making more competitive decisions.

    As the next step of our project, we aim to get production and process parameters directly and online from the machines in SFC (Production Data Collection) with CoralReef. We will make the process of data collection from production independent of the personnel's declaration, and we will manage our resources and capacity to higher levels of our OEE values ​​based on an accurate data archive.

    • What level did İletişim Yazılım meet our needs?

    İletişim Yazılım has proved to be a consultant and a solution partner with us until we reached a certain level.

    Madosan Shelf Systems will not limit the digital transformation process to the field of production management. We will carry out many different processes such as document management, quality standards management, project management, CRM, workflow and meeting management, suggestion and survey management to a digital platform. In this context, we aim to include the ManageMind BPM solution of Iletisim Software by the end of this year.
