    Smart Factory Solutions will be in Çerkezköy soon!

    “Smart Factory Solutions and MES for Successful ERP Applications”

    20 April 2017

    While the importance and efforts of developed countries to smart factories in order to increase productivity and reduce costs in factories increased, the concept of smart factory started to be implemented in our country. Since 23 years; In international competition, all of Turkey "Intelligent Business Solutions", aiming to carry İletişim Yazılım, organized by the "Successful Smart Factory Solutions and MES to ERP Application" event will be held at the Industrial Area Directorate third of May 24, 2017 on Wednesday in Çerkezköy. The event, which consists of six sessions covering Industry 4.0, MES (Manufacturing Execution System), smart factory solutions, success stories and academic approach, will bring together the different disciplines responsible for production in enterprises that want to capture the production understanding of the future. Within the scope of the program, Anadolu Yarn General Manager Tuna Gürarı will share the work they have realized and the success they have achieved.
